IT TRAINING AND COACHING

Many startups in their way of growing fast and minimizing  risk  decide to hire us to support their teams and make them highly productive.

We meet the team, identify their needs and work closely with them (elbow by elbow) in order to teach them best practices, help them to solve daily problems and make them adopt good procedures.

We call it “learning by doing”. In our large experience we found this methodology helps to:

  1. Speed up production
  2. Detect and solve bugs in early stages
  3. Build a better and more stable code
